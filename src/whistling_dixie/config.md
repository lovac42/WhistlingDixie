# Whistling Dixie: Redo Failed Cards

Pretty self explainatory...  
Or Just don't touch it.


## Type:
checks: All OR (Type AND Queue)  
0=new, 1=lrn, 2=rev, 3=relrn (V2)  
e.g. [0,2,3]  

## Queue:
checks: All OR (Type AND Queue)  
0=new, 1=(re)lrn, 2=rev, 3=day (re)lrn, 4=filter (V2)
e.g. [0,2,3]
